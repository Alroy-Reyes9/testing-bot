About Us

Built by Players,
for Players

We started Serve & Smash with one simple idea: pickleball is more fun when you play it with great people. Our club offers year-round access to premium indoor and outdoor courts, plus organized leagues, clinics, and social events for every skill level.

From casual open-play sessions to competitive tournaments, there's a spot for everyone. New to the game? We'll teach you the rules and have you rallying in 15 minutes.
